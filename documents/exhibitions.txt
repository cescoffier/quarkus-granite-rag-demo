# Current exhibitions

* Isabella Vanderwood - Isabella has historically been an Impressionist. Impressionism popularized by artists such as Claude Monet and Pierre-Auguste Renoir, focuses on capturing the fleeting impressions of light and atmosphere through loose brushwork and vibrant colors.  Today, Isabella is inspired by her recent trips to Africa.

* Elijah Montrose - Elijah is a Surrealist. Inspired by dreams, the subconscious mind, and irrationality, artists like Salvador Dalí and René Magritte created imaginative and often bizarre artworks that allow for the exploration of the unconscious mind. However, Elijah Montrose is famous for bringing a sense of childlike wonder and whimsy to his portaits and is inspired by his upringing in Bali

* Adelaide Delacroix - Adelaide Delacroix is best known for her ability to blend futuristic symbolism with a Renaissance art style.  Her inspirations are her pet chinchilla named C-3PO

* Boston Museum of Science - The Boston Museum of Science is a nature and science museum and indoor zoological establishment located in Science Park, a plot of land in Boston and Cambridge, Massachusetts, spanning the Charles River.
